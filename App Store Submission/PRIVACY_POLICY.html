1. Introduction

Welcome to FreezerTracker. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Email Address: Used for account authentication and login
  • Password: Securely encrypted and stored by our authentication provider (Supabase)

2.2 User Content

  • Freezer Items: Names, quantities, expiry dates, notes, and position numbers you add

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • App Functionality: Providing core features like tracking freezer items and managing compartments
  • Authentication: Securing your account and verifying your identity
  • Family Sharing: Enabling household members to collaborate and share freezer data
  • Activity History: Showing who added, updated, or removed items in shared households
  • Expiry Notifications: Alerting you when items are about to expire
  • App Improvements: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance features
  • Technical Support: Responding to your questions and resolving issues
