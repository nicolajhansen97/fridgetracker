- ‚úÖ Show real data (realistic item names)

**Caption Style:**
- Font: San Francisco (Apple's system font) or similar
- Size: Large enough to read on small displays
- Color: White text on gradient, or gradient text on white
- Position: Top third of screen
- Length: 3-7 words maximum

**Example Captions:**

## üí° Tips for Great Screenshots

1. **Use Real Devices:** Screenshots from real devices look better than simulator
2. **Natural Lighting:** If photographing a real device, use natural light
3. **Status Bar:** Show full battery, good signal (use Xcode simulator features)
4. **Consistency:** Use same device frame for all screenshots
5. **Tell a Story:** Screenshots should flow logically (onboarding ‚Üí usage ‚Üí benefits)
6. **Localization:** Create separate screenshots for different languages if targeting multiple markets
7. **Test on Devices:** View your screenshots on actual phones to ensure readability
